After leaving WentUrc, the divine did not immediately create another world.

First, the divine let the heart become quiet. The thoughts that had once collided and covered one another returned to their places, like scattered stars finding an invisible map.

"Beauty cannot merely appear," the divine whispered. "It must be held, understood, and preserved with tenderness."

In that second contemplation, rhythm was given to thought. Imagination received boundaries. Dreams were taught when to rise, when to rest, and when to step aside.

When the first stable intention took form, **Revaea** unfolded.

Revaea did not surge out of chaos. It opened from an arranged will, like a score already written before the first note was heard. The sky received a constant blue, deep but never dark, bright but never harsh. Stars moved along quiet paths and yielded to one another in the night.

There were still dreams here, but they no longer tore at each other. They gathered, answered, and slowly joined into stories.

Revaea was the perfect dream: not because it had no sorrow, but because every sorrow could find a place where it might be understood.
