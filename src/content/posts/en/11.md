Inside IGCrystal, there are names that do not belong to the outer shell alone.

**IGCyukira** and **IGCyukito** are not simple opposites. They are two ways the inner self survives and speaks. One gathers softness, memory, and the wish to be held. The other gathers form, resolve, and the wish to keep moving even when the heart is tired.

Together, they make an inner weather.

Sometimes they argue. Sometimes they shelter one another. Sometimes one must step forward because the other has no strength left to face the day.

Their story is not about choosing one true self and abandoning the other.

It is about learning that a life may contain more than one voice, and that harmony does not require any voice to disappear.
