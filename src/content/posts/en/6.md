**Xirayu** carries the softness of rain after a long season of silence.

She is tied to memory, not as a fixed archive, but as a living current. What she remembers does not remain unchanged. It ripples, blurs, and returns with new tenderness each time it is touched.

To meet Xirayu is to stand beneath a sky that has decided not to accuse anyone. Her rain does not wash the past away. It loosens the dust around it, allowing buried feelings to breathe again.

Her story belongs to those who have forgotten how to grieve clearly.

She teaches that remembrance is not the opposite of healing. Sometimes it is the first door through which healing enters.
