**IZCherry** belongs to the moment when a blossom leaves the branch.

She is not defined by loss, but by motion. The falling petal is not merely ending its bloom. It is entering the air, catching light, and drawing a brief path that could never exist while it remained still.

IZCherry's beauty is quick, vivid, and difficult to keep. That is part of its meaning.

She reminds Revaea that not everything precious must become permanent. Some things heal because they arrive at exactly the right instant, touch the heart, and then continue onward.

Her story is full of color, but the color is never careless.

It is the brightness of someone who knows how fragile brightness can be, and loves it anyway.
