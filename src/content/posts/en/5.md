**IGCrystal** is a figure of quiet structure.

Her nature is not coldness, although it may look that way from a distance. It is the discipline of a crystal: transparent, restrained, and unwilling to let chaos injure what should be protected.

She observes before she reaches out. She judges carefully because she knows that careless certainty can become another form of harm. Around her, emotions are not denied, but refracted. Pain is given shape. Love is given a boundary that keeps it from burning itself away.

In Revaea, IGCrystal represents the difficult kindness of clarity.

She does not promise that every wound can be made beautiful. She promises only to hold the wound in a form that can be seen, named, and carried without breaking the one who bears it.
